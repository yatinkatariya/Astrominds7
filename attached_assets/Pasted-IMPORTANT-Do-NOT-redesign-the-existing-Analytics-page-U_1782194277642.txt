====================================================
ANALYTICS PAGE OBJECTIVE
====================================================

The Analytics page should act as the Research & Intelligence Center of the platform.

It must help users understand:

1. How AQI changes over time.
2. Which pollutants are causing pollution.
3. How weather affects AQI.
4. How biomass burning affects HCHO.
5. How HCHO affects AQI.
6. Which cities are most polluted.
7. Which regions are HCHO hotspots.
8. What insights can be extracted from the data.
